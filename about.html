BACKGROUND

The magazine was initially created back in 2010 under the name – “That's it!” and was distributed throughout Port Elizabeth, South Africa. It was later decided that the magazine be repurposed for Venda and distributed in Thohoyandou.

ABOUT THE MAGAZINE

The month of Spring brought much more than just a change of season in 2017... 

Henda! Venda Commuter Magazine launched the 1st Edition of the magazine and distribution was made possible by the Thohoyandou Taxi Association.

The magazine consists of advertising, editorials, discount offers, competitions, community notices and classifieds.

OBJECTIVES of the MAG

  1. The magazine’s main focus is to inform passengers on the whereabouts of their local businesses as well as any discounts or specials that may be on offer.
  2. The need for business exposure is increasingly becoming more needed as competing companies arrive in Thohoyandouas a result of expansion in the area. 
  3. We hope to promote local businesses by advertising and marketing. It is our aim to  encourage communities to support local businesses, as this ensures growth and creates more opportunities in their hometowns 
  4. Henda Venda offers advertisers an opportunity to reach out to their customers in a way that was not possible in the past.

DISTRIBUTION

10,000 copies *(Average) printed in a6, full colour, gloss on a monthly basis and distributed to commuters via mini-bus taxis of the Thohoyandou Taxi Association.
